Understanding Sexual Harassment

What is Sexual Harassment?

  • Unwanted sexual advances or requests for sexual favors
  • Verbal or physical conduct of a sexual nature
  • Creating a hostile or intimidating environment
  • Using position of power to coerce or manipulate

Prevention & Protection

  • Know your rights and boundaries
  • Document incidents and keep records
  • Report to appropriate authorities
  • Seek support from trusted individuals

Support Resources

  • 24/7 National Sexual Assault Hotline: 800-656-HOPE
  • Online Chat Support: RAINN.org
  • Local counseling and advocacy services
  • Legal aid and victim services

Understanding Social Media Peer Pressure

Peer pressure in social media influences behavior, choices, and self-perception, both positively and negatively. It can be direct (encouragement from friends or influencers) or indirect (seeing trends and feeling the need to conform). While it can promote good habits, it also leads to issues like anxiety, risky behavior, and excessive spending.

To handle social media peer pressure, think before acting, limit screen time, follow positive influences, be yourself, and seek support if needed. Social media should be a space for connection, not stress.

Understanding & Preventing Cyberbullying

What is Cyberbullying?

  • Online harassment through social media, messaging, or email
  • Sharing harmful or false content about someone
  • Making threats or intimidating others online
  • Creating fake profiles to harm someone's reputation

Prevention & Protection

  • Use strong privacy settings on social media
  • Think before sharing personal information
  • Keep evidence of cyberbullying incidents
  • Block and report abusive accounts

Support Resources

  • Cyberbullying Hotline: 1-800-273-8255
  • Online Support: StopBullying.gov
  • School counseling services
  • Digital safety organizations

Understanding & Preventing Harmful Pranks

What are Harmful Pranks?

  • Actions that cause physical or emotional harm
  • Pranks that damage property or reputation
  • Social media challenges that promote dangerous behavior
  • Unwanted recordings or sharing of embarrassing moments

Prevention & Protection

  • Think before participating in trends or challenges
  • Consider the consequences of your actions
  • Respect others' boundaries and dignity
  • Report dangerous pranks to authorities

Support Resources

  • School Counseling Services
  • Anti-Bullying Hotline: 1-800-273-8255
  • Online Safety Resources
  • Legal Aid for Victims

Understanding & Managing Social Media Addiction

Signs of Social Media Addiction

  • Constantly checking social media platforms
  • Feeling anxious when unable to access social media
  • Losing track of time while online
  • Neglecting real-life relationships and responsibilities
  • Comparing yourself to others online frequently
  • Using social media to escape from problems

Digital Wellness Strategies

  • Set specific times for social media use
  • Use app timers and screen time limits
  • Turn off non-essential notifications
  • Create phone-free zones and times
  • Practice mindful browsing habits
  • Find offline activities and hobbies

Support & Resources

  • Digital Wellness Support Groups
  • Mental Health Counseling Services
  • Screen Time Management Apps
  • Digital Detox Programs
  • Mindfulness and Meditation Resources
